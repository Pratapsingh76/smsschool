School Structure

Educational Journey at SMS School

From the very first day of school to board exams — a well-structured 12-year academic journey.

Stage 1

Primary School

Classes I – V
  • Activity-based learning
  • English & Hindi Literacy
  • Basic Sciences & Maths
  • Arts, Music & PE
  • No formal board exams
Age: 6 – 11 years
Stage 2

Middle School

Classes VI – VIII
  • Subject-wise teaching
  • Science, Maths, SST
  • Computer Education
  • Third Language
  • Half/Full Yearly exams
Age: 11 – 14 years
Stage 3

Secondary

Classes IX – X
  • CBSE Board Syllabus
  • Practical Exams
  • Mock Test Series
  • Career Counselling
  • Class X Board Exam
Age: 14 – 16 years
Stage 4

Sr. Secondary

Classes XI – XII
  • Science / Commerce streams
  • Entrance Exam Prep
  • Lab Practicals
  • Higher-Ed Guidance
  • Class XII Board Exam
Age: 16 – 18 years
